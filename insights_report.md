# Movie Success Analysis — Insights Report

## Objective
Analyze movie data to understand what factors influence success.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Ratings vs Revenue
Higher IMDb ratings do not guarantee higher revenue.
Some lower-rated movies generate significant earnings.

### 2. Directors Impact
Certain directors consistently produce high-grossing movies,
suggesting reputation plays a role in commercial success.

### 3. Runtime vs Rating
No strong relationship between movie length and rating.

### 4. Votes vs Ratings
Movies with more votes tend to have more stable ratings,
but popularity does not always mean higher quality.

### 5. Trends Over Time
IMDb ratings have remained relatively stable over the years.

### 6. Revenue Concentration
A small number of movies generate most of the revenue,
showing that success is highly concentrated.

---

## Conclusion
Movie success is influenced by multiple factors, and no single variable
guarantees high revenue. Commercial success is often unpredictable.
